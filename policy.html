Privacy Policy

At SIDS India, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our digital marketing services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect personal and business information that you voluntarily provide through our website, contact forms, email communications, phone calls, social media platforms, or business consultations. This information may include:

  • Full name and designation
  • Company or business name
  • Business profile and industry details
  • Email address and contact number
  • Website URL and social media profiles
  • Project requirements, marketing objectives, and business goals
  • Billing or invoicing information (where applicable)

In addition, we automatically collect certain technical information when you visit our website, including:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Device information
  • Operating system
  • Referral source
  • Pages visited and time spent on the website
  • Cookies and website usage analytics

This information helps us improve website performance, user experience, and the quality of our services.

2. How We Use Your Information

SIDS India uses the information collected for legitimate business purposes, including:

  • To understand your business requirements and respond to inquiries.
  • To prepare proposals, quotations, and marketing strategies.
  • To provide digital marketing, branding, website development, and related services.
  • To communicate regarding ongoing projects and customer support.
  • To improve our website, services, and customer experience.
  • To send service updates, invoices, newsletters, or promotional communications (where permitted).
  • To comply with applicable legal and regulatory requirements.

3. Data Sharing

SIDS India does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party.

Your information may only be shared with trusted partners and service providers who assist us in operating our business, including:

  • Website hosting providers
  • Cloud storage services
  • Analytics platforms
  • CRM software
  • Payment processing providers
  • Email communication services

These service providers are contractually obligated to maintain the confidentiality and security of your information.

We may also disclose information where required by applicable law, legal proceedings, government authorities, or when necessary to protect the rights, property, or safety of SIDS India, our clients, or others.

4. Data Retention and Security

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and maintain business records.

SIDS India implements industry-standard administrative, technical, and organizational security measures, including secure servers, encrypted communications, restricted access controls, and regular monitoring to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

While we strive to safeguard your data, no method of electronic transmission or storage can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

5. Your Rights

Subject to applicable laws, you may have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal information.
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Request deletion of your personal data.
  • Restrict or object to certain processing activities.
  • Withdraw consent for marketing communications at any time.
  • Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details provided below.
